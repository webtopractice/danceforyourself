About Dharvi

Dharvi Awasthi is a dancer and choreographer from Bangalore. A relentless artist with exceptional technique and a warm, patient teaching style. Dharvi leads classes for children, adult beginners, ladies' fitness-focused dance, and runs choreography workshops for performers of all ages.

Teaching approach

Friendly, encouraging and structured. Classes balance technical fundamentals, creativity and performance confidence — ideal for students preparing for shows, building fitness, or discovering dance for the first time.
